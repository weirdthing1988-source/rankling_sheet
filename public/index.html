Cohort Bond

Distributed Character

The Brass and formed troopers occupy one Medium space, use one turn and share Formation HP and conditions. Troopers do not gain independent attacks unless a feature explicitly commands one.

Dependent Troopers

A trooper can eat, rest, maintain equipment and follow a simple instruction, but cannot establish its own combat objective. Without an instruction it seeks its recognised leader or returns to the Brass.

Detached Trooper

Only one trooper may be deliberately detached at a time. It occupies a Small space, uses its own AC and HP, and acts immediately after its temporary leader.

Leaderless Behaviour

A trooper follows its temporary leader, protects itself from immediate danger, returns to the visible Brass, searches the Brass's last known location, then shelters and waits for authority.
